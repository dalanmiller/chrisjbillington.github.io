Australian COVID vaccination rollout

Below are daily and cumulative COVID-19 vaccine doses administered in Australia, as of the latest federal figures. These are compared to the average rate required for 40 million doses to be administered by the end of October, which is approximately what Australia is targeting.

Details

  • Daily doses have been smoothed using 2-day Gaussian smoothing. This allows the general trend to be more visible despite large day-to-day fluctuations in the data.
  • If the federal government has not released data recently, the most recent daily rate (after smoothing) from reported data is used to project the missing data. This is shown separately in the daily doses plot. The plot of cumulative doses does not include any projected numbers.

    • Source for dose numbers: covidlive.com.au
